about factorie

Factorie is an industrial IoT-driven intelligent ecosystem that manages your production planning, increases production & machine efficiency, monitors maintenance activities & energy consumption, helps bolster workforce efficiency and assesses the financial viability of your factory.

something for
everyone

Company Owner

  • Create production plans & timelines for each task.
  • Measure profits from every project, client & machine.
  • Improve plant efficiency using customizable KPIs.
  • Monitor energy utilization by the plant & machines.
  • Create role-based training modules for your employees & track their progress.

Production Managers

  • Create production plans & timelines for each task.
  • Stay on-top of production process with live updates.
  • Monitor & improve the efficiency of machines & operators.
  • Anticipate machine maintenance using historic reports & deep analytics.
  • Create role-based training modules for your employees & track their progress.

Supervisor

  • Save time by remotely monitoring the progress.
  • Resolves issues promptly by quickly escalating alerts to the concerned department.
  • Ensure smooth production by identifing & fixing issues.

Operators

  • Stop worring about filling manual reports.
  • Always stay on the target by keeping track of the progress.
  • Rise through the ranks using detailed performance reports and internal training modules.

the team

Jitendra Kumar Penikelapati

Founder & CEO

With academic achievements in Instrumentation Engineering and an experience of over a decade in designing, development and maintenance in the area of Machine Automation, Jitendra adds a perspective of pragmatism to everything he does. A perspective that helped him observe gaps in the manufacturing sector that needs urgent attention to tackle unpredictable timelines, untracked machine efficiency, people performance and profitability.

FactorIE is the brainchild of Jitendra that aims to fill the aforementioned gaps. With FactorIE, he wishes to make manufacturing segment a happy segment.
